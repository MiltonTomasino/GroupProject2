Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you deliver?

A: We will offer delivery service through UberEats, DoorDash, and Fantuan

Q: Why make a Quicky Account?

A: We offer discounts and promotions for our loyal customers who become Quicky Members. You can earn points that you can redeem
for free items off the menu like our famous Dim Sum and Chow Mein. Also becoming a Quick Member lets you to quickly order food online
and pick up an order at the store.
